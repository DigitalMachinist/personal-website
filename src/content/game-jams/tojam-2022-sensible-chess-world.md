Built for TOJam 2022: All Twogether Now, where the theme was "That's not supposed to be there" as a team of 4. We took something familiar and mixed in modern game mechanics -- "sensible" updates to a classic game.

At a game jam, the biggest win is making a 5-minute gameplay experience that surprises and delights, and this game was a riot to build and to have other jammers play. I'm also impressed we managed to jam together a decent interpretation of chess rules so quickly, nevermind battle royale mode!
