Built for TOJam 2023: We Lost Count, where the theme was "You have to leave something behind" as a team of 3, with some other devs from Rivalry's games team. In our take on the theme, you play as a robot with detachable you can place and then use from afar to solve puzzles in this Portal-esqe brain teaser.

We felt very good about the game design space this one gave us for having such simple components to work with. While the demo is only 3 short levels, this felt like it could easily expand into more and be very enjoyable!
